Bleach

Bleach is a Japanese Manga written by Tite Kubo. The story revolves around a boy named Ichigo Kurosaki after he has obtained the powers of a Soul Reaper--adeath personification similar to Soul Reaper--from another Soul Reaper,Rukia Kuchiki.
The story revolves around how the newfound powers force Ichigo to defend humanity against evil sprits and other evil creatures.
Although initially reluctant to accept his responsibility, he takes her place, and during this time they discover that a few classmates are spiritually aware and have their own powers: Uryū Ishida a Quincy uses spiritual particles, Orihime Inoue has a group of protective spirits called Shun Shun Rikka and Yasutora Sado has strength equal to the Hollows encased in his arm.

Ichigo & Rukia

When Ichigo and Rukia first met, they didn't understand each other very well, but only after Ichigo has borrowed Rukia's Soul Reaper powers to kill a Hollow, from their on there friendship started to bloom.
